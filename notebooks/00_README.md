This is a Data Scientist project made back at my Masters Degree years now being adapted for Machine Learning Engineering, meaning, bridging Research Level to Production Level.

1- The Business task is based on the prediction of Airbnb Price listings in New York City(NYC) in 2019.
2- The Machine Learning task is expected to be Supervised Learning.
3- Unit of observation: One Airbnb listing
4- Target Variable or Dependent Variable: Price or if feasible, take log_price in consideration
5- Prediction Moment: Expected at time of listing, meaning latency needs to be considered
6- Success Metrics: Technical metrics include R2, MSE, RMSE, MAE, MAPE.
7- Business Metrics: Average Price Error in main currency
8- Constraints include: Interpretability, simple deployment and low latency
9- Leakage Rsiks in consideration are notably availability and reviews that may happen at the time of listing.
